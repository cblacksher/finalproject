Karl Hinkley

NoWear BMX was started as a passion of mine to get kids off the streets and on the ramps. I know as well as the next guy how easy it is to end up in trouble in a small midwestern town. So I started this company as a way to give back to the community that has helped me to grow as a person, and as a leader. I hope you enjoy our product offerings.
